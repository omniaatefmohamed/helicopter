Your Experience (Description)

See the famous landmarks of Dubai in the air between skies, depart from the Jumeirah Heliport of Helidubai, and witness the spectacular views of the Palm Jumeirah, the Burj Al Arab, and the World Islands by The Palm 17 minute helicopter ride Dubai.

Some of the record-holding buildings, such as the tallest Burj Khalifa building and the tallest hotel, JW Marriot Hotel, get a closer look. Be fascinated by the spectacular sights of the Jumeirah coastline, the iconic Port Rashid, and be awed by the largest flag of the UAE as the tour begins.

Trip Instructions / Essentials

The tour starts at the Dubai Marina; cruise around the Marina as you marvel at Dubai’s modern skyline, the yacht docks and the urban lifestyle. You will go past the Yacht club, the five star beach hotels and see the fabulous JBR (Jumeirah Beach Residence) walk. You will then head to Ain Dubai to discover the Bluewaters Island located off the Jumeirah Beach coast. The Island features residential apartments and the famous Ain Dubai Ferris Wheel. Later, experience JBR from the sea and get to see the amazing waterfront community. You will also see the iconic Atlantis Hotel, one of the modern wonders that Dubai has to offer. Burj Al Arab is also a must see sight with its unique ship sail design providing an excellent photo opportunity. You will then head back to Dubai Marina at the end of the tour.
